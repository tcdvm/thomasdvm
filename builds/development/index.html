Hi there.

My name is Thomas and I work at the University of Tennesse Veterinary Medical Center and also at Village Veterinary Medical Center (apparently I have a thing for working at veterinary medical centers). I'm interested in ocular disease and surgery as well as data analysis and visualization.

I like teaching and designing/writing teaching materials and so this website has some handouts and slides I've produced for some talks I've given as well as references to some research and other fun projects that I've dabbled in.

Below is a sample of the thrilling and exciting material available here.

  • Tackling glaucoma (lecture)
  • Non-healing corneal ulcers (lecture)
  • Tear production in horses (research)
  • Intern magnets ("fun" stuff)
